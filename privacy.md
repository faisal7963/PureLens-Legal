# Privacy Policy for PureLens

Effective Date: June 2026

## 1. Overview

PureLens is designed as a privacy-first creative studio for video editing, photo editing, project-based editing, and audio processing.

Your media remains under your control. PureLens processes your content primarily on your device and does not sell your personal information.

---

## 2. Media Processing

PureLens may access photos, videos, audio files, and project files that you explicitly choose to edit.

These files are used solely to provide requested editing features, including:

- Video enhancement
- Color grading
- LUT processing
- Log conversion
- Project editing
- Audio Studio features
- Photo Studio features
- Background removal
- Subject selection
- Image enhancement
- Exporting edited content

Your media is not uploaded to PureLens servers.

---

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

PureLens does not collect:

- Personal photos
- Personal videos
- Audio recordings
- Project files
- Exported media

Your edited content remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

---

## 4. Advertising

The free version of PureLens may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect certain device and advertising-related information in accordance with Google's privacy policies, including:

- Device identifiers
- Advertising identifiers
- App interaction data
- Diagnostic information

PureLens does not receive your personal media content from AdMob.

---

## 5. Analytics

PureLens may use analytics services such as Firebase Analytics to understand:

- Feature usage
- Application performance
- Stability and crash reports
- General engagement metrics

Analytics data does not include your photos, videos, audio files, or project content.

---

## 6. Subscriptions

PureLens offers optional premium subscriptions through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple.

PureLens does not store your payment information.

---

## 7. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Where required, PureLens requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before accessing advertising identifiers.

You may change this permission at any time in iOS Settings.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

PureLens may use:

- Apple StoreKit
- Google AdMob
- Firebase Analytics
- Apple Photos Framework
- Apple AVFoundation

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

---

## 9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data and maintain the security of the application.

Because media processing occurs primarily on-device, your content remains under your control.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

PureLens is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

---

## 12. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support information provided within the App Store listing.
